As an intern

Internships are a great opportunity to learn relevant skills and knowledge for your industry, so it's important to show gratitude by thanking your employers. Thanking them can leave a good impression and increase your chances of getting a full-time employment offer. In addition, remember that your manager is likely to ask others to review your performance. This makes it vital to thank your colleagues just as you thank your managers and supervisors. If you work with different teams during your internship, remember to thank all of them in person if possible.
